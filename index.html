About

Tundra Tech Talks is a monthly social meetup targeted at software engineers, engineering managers, product owners, and a variety of tech roles.

If you work in or have any interest in tech, live in the Ithaca area, and want to hang out with some cool folks, then come swing by!

Join us!

We meet the last Tuesday of every month, 6:30 PM. Join our Discord here:
